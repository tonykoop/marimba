# Drawing Brief

Current drawing authority: future. This packet has no fabrication-authority CAD, DXF, or reviewed dimensioned drawings yet.

Generated images and sketches are concept/reference only. They must not be used for cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, toolpaths, hammer centers, aperture centers, or resonator lengths.

## Future Authority Drawings

### D001: P0 Bench Coupon Assembly

Purpose: prove one key, hammer, bar, aperture, gasket, resonator, and platen clamp.

Must show:

- Pivot locations.
- Adjustable hammer strike point.
- Bar support node positions.
- Aperture plate and gasket stack.
- Resonator height adjustment.
- Clamp throw and hard stop.

Authority target: first fabrication drawing after dimensions are selected from available stock.

### D002: Three-Face Hammer Head

Purpose: define the manually indexed P0 hammer head and later P1 selector-compatible head.

Must show:

- Face insert pockets.
- Indexing detent geometry.
- Retention method for soft and hard faces.
- Strike radius and clearance envelope.

Authority target: CAD or measured drawing after face material samples are chosen.

### D003: 13-Note Voice Cassette

Purpose: define a removable C4-C5 tone-bar and aperture module.

Must show:

- Bar centerline map.
- Node support positions.
- Aperture centerline map.
- Gasket pockets.
- Tapered registration features.
- Platen contact surfaces.

Authority target: future CAD/DXF only after P0 and early P1 measurements.

### D004: Adjustable Resonator Bed

Purpose: define independent, adjustable pipe support.

Must show:

- Pipe centerlines.
- Standoff and spring locations.
- Adjustment range.
- Alignment pins or V-blocks.
- Clearance for straight, bent, and downward-facing pipe experiments.

Authority target: future CAD/DXF after aperture tests show the needed pipe bank strategy.

### D005: Platen Lever and Interlock

Purpose: make the cassette clamping safe and repeatable.

Must show:

- Lever throw.
- Cam or toggle geometry.
- Over-center or lock position.
- Adjustable hard stop.
- Guarding and cassette-removal interlock.

Authority target: future mechanical drawing after P0 clamp force is known.

### D006: Hammer Selector Rail

Purpose: define the left-side selector that changes hammer faces across the row.

Must show:

- Rest-state engagement.
- 120-degree index positions.
- Detent locations.
- Clearance during hammer travel.
- Manual override or service release.

Authority target: defer until one-note and three-note selector tests pass.

# Image-Gen Prompt - Marimba

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, node locations, resonator lengths, or CNC toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic studio product photograph of a finished 37-bar C3-C6 marimba with warm African Padauk bars arranged chromatically over matched vertical quarter-wave resonator tubes, a clean CNC-made frame, visible black suspension cord at the bar nodes, and subtle parabolic underside arching hinted at on the bass bars. Show the instrument at realistic concert scale on a clean neutral studio background, three-quarter product-photography angle, soft directional studio lighting, approximately 50mm lens, high detail, natural wood grain, brushed or satin metal resonators, no logos.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic image of the marimba staged in a quiet makerspace rehearsal room, with a player standing behind it holding two yarn mallets, warm daylight from high windows, a few measuring tools and tuner notebook off to the side to suggest prototype validation, Padauk bars glowing against the metal resonator bank, realistic scale and reach, natural shadows, documentary product-build atmosphere.

## Detail/Macro Prompt

Macro photorealistic close-up of one Padauk marimba bar suspended at its nodal cord holes above a resonator tube, showing smooth CNC-surfaced edges, a subtle underside tuning arch, rubber or cord isolation, and the round resonator mouth below, shallow depth of field, crisp wood grain and satin metal texture, soft raking studio light.

## Negative/Avoid

No text, no watermarks, no brand marks, no extra bars or impossible note layouts, no cut-ready dimensions, no toolpath overlays, no floating resonators, anatomically plausible hands if a player is shown.
